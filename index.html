Knowledge


In the beginning were the Lectures. The first one was about PHYSICAL COMPUTING, then ARDUINO came into play, after that was time for ELECTRONIC MUSIC and SYNTH, later on we introduced the disability theme and eventually we talked about DOCUMENTATION. During this lectures we were hosted most of the times by Politecnico of Turin. We used to share our knowledge all together like a hippy community. Throughout this period of time we invited great PROFESSORS from all over the engineering world.

Design


We call the second part of the process: DESIGN. We developed in depth our concepts. Then we ran out of the classroom to gather the stuff we needed like boards, sensor, musical instruments, wood and others prototyping material. Afterwards we made sensors and board talk to each other through software libraries, scripts, sketches and patches. We’re carrying out most of the work, prototyping at Turin’s Fablab

Performance


After not enough rehearsing the show is on! A lot of people on stage, one director, one sound technician and loads of MAGIC and FUN! We are ADO. Stay tuned for more dates :)
